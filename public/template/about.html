Let's face it : college textbooks are insanely expensive. And, at the end of the term, the bookstore gives students virtually nothing back for their books. We believe in a better way for students to sell their textbooks quickly, conveniently, and for a fair price. In spite of our substantial size and reach, we remain grounded by our company's core values, which have guided us through the ups and downs of the bookselling industry. Each and every employee's love of books drives us forward.

We save you time and money by searching every major catalog online, and letting you know which booksellers are offering the best prices and selection. When you find a book you like, you can buy it directly from the original seller; we never charge a markup.

MISSION

Our mission is to be the world's best destination for readers, a place that fosters a culture of reading and connects people with the books they'll love.
